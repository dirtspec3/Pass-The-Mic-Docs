Pass The Mic – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. What We Collect

  • Server and Channel IDs – to identify where to run sessions.
  • User IDs – to manage speaking turns and mute/unmute actions.
  • Command Usage Logs – timestamped records of which commands were run and by whom, for debugging and analytics.
  • No voice or text content is recorded or stored.

2. How We Use Data

  • Operate core features (e.g., timing speakers, updating queues).
  • Monitor performance, detect abuse, and fix bugs.
  • Gather anonymous statistics (server count, active sessions) to improve the Service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Limited data may be shared with:

  • Our hosting provider (for processing) under strict confidentiality.
  • Law enforcement if required by law.

4. Data Retention

Logs are kept for up to 90 days and then permanently deleted.

5. Security

We employ industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, role-based access) to protect stored data. No method is 100% secure, but we strive for reasonable safeguards.

6. Your Choices

  • Remove the bot to stop all data collection.
  • Email us to request deletion of command logs tied to your User ID.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date. Continuing to use the Service means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

Privacy questions? Email privacy@passthemic.app.
